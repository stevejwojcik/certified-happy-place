Keep the Good Times Rolling.

Don’t stop movin’ and groovin.’ With the 24-hour Roadside Assistance† program available during your limited warranty period, you’ll never miss a beat when you have to go places. Find you inner peace knowing that wherever you go, you’ve got a ride.

No More Shadows of Doubt.

You no longer have to be afraid of buying a used vehicle. Your Certified Pre-Owned vehicle is backed with an Extended New-Car Bumper-to-Bumper Limited Warranty for 12 Months/12,000 miles.† Also get an extension to the original Powertrain Limited Warranty† for a total of 6-years/100,000-miles, so you can confidently drive far away to your.. ahem.. not-so-haunted lake house.

When Things Change, You Can Change Your Mind Too.

We can’t predict the future. But what we can do is give you a chance to make sure you find the vehicle that perfectly fits your lifestyle. That’s why every Certified Pre-Owned vehicle has a 3-day/150-mile Exchange Policy† that lets you bring back your purchase to your dealer and exchange it for something that better fits your needs — and your band equipment.

Behold! A history report fit for royalty.

Only reliable pre-owned steeds are Certified. Every Chevrolet, Buick or GMC Certified Pre-Owned vehicle comes with a detailed vehicle history report, which includes past problems such as major accidents, fire or title issues. So you can verify that the vehicle you’re going to buy is worthy to take you to lands far and wide.

IMPORTANT RECALL INFORMATION: Before a Certified Pre-Owned vehicle is listed or sold, GM requires dealers to complete all safety recalls. However, because even the best processes can break down, we encourage you to check the recall status of any vehicle at recalls.gm.com.

Hand-Selected Vehicles. A Breed Apart.

All dogs are good dogs, but not all used vehicles are Certified. It takes something special to be a winner. Our vehicles are hand-selected, and have to be under 6 years old and 75,000 miles to make the cut. Our title is earned and that’s what makes them a treat to drive.
